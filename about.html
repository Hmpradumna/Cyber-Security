SolarWinds Supply Chain Attack

A sophisticated nation-state attack that compromised over 18,000 organizations by injecting malware into legitimate software updates.

  • Vector: Supply Chain Compromise
  • Impact: Global Infrastructure Exposure
  • Lesson: Multi-factor authentication is not enough; verify binary integrity.

Colonial Pipeline Ransomware

Ransomware attackers shut down a major fuel pipeline on the US East Coast, causing widespread shortages and panic.

  • Vector: Credential Stuffing (VPN)
  • Impact: Critical Energy Infrastructure
  • Lesson: Patch administrative interfaces and monitor legacy VPN accounts.

WannaCry Global Outbreak

A massive ransomware attack that exploited a Windows vulnerability (EternalBlue), affecting hospitals and businesses in 150 countries.

  • Vector: Network Worm / SMB Exploit
  • Impact: Healthcare & Logistics Shutdown
  • Lesson: Rapid patch management is critical for survival.
