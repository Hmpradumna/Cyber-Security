Social Engineering Call

Why spend weeks hacking a firewall when you can just ask someone for their password? This is the core philosophy of social engineering.

The Art of "Vishing"

Voice Phishing (Vishing) uses the telephone system to conduct social engineering. Attackers use emotional manipulation—fear, urgency, or curiosity—to trick victims into revealing sensitive information.

  • Impersonation: Attackers often pose as IT support, bank officials, or law enforcement.
  • Caller ID Spoofing: They make it look like the call is coming from a trusted local number.

Defense Tip: If someone calls you claiming to be from a trusted organization and asks for data, hang up. Call them back on the official number you find on their website.
